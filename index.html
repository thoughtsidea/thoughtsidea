FAQ

+

What types of projects do you offer?

We offer a wide range of projects, including mini projects and main projects, across all departments. Whether it's software development, electronics, mechanical engineering, or any other field, we have customized project solutions to suit your needs.

+

Can I choose my own project topic?

Absolutely! We encourage students to explore their interests and passions. You can propose your project topic, and our expert team will work closely with you to refine and develop it into a successful project.

+

How long does it take to complete a project?

The project duration varies depending on its complexity and scope. Typically, mini projects can be completed within a week, while main projects may require a few weeks. Rest assured, we will provide a clear timeline and ensure timely project delivery.

+

Do you provide guidance and support during the project?

Yes, we provide comprehensive guidance and support throughout the project. Our experienced mentors will assist you at every stage, offering technical expertise, resources, and regular progress reviews to ensure a successful outcome.

+

Can I showcase the project in my academic institution?

Absolutely! We encourage students to showcase their projects in their academic institutions. We provide complete project documentation and support, enabling you to present your work confidently and receive recognition for your efforts.

+

When do I pay?

For most projects, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the project, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs. We understand that this is a big investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible. We accept checks, PayPal, and all major credit cards.
